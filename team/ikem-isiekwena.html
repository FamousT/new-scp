Ikem Isiekwena

Managing Partner, SimmonsCooper Partners

  • +234 8033894864
  • V-card

Ikem leads our Strategic Business Unit which encompasses Anti-trust/Competition, Mergers & Acquisition, Private Equity and Energy team

Ikem Isiekwena serves as a Managing Partner of the firm, where he plays a vital role in supervising and overseeing the firm's strategic direction to ensure its ongoing growth and success. His responsibilities extend to the management of the firm's clients, personnel, and practices. He steers the firm towards strategic excellence, overseeing its direction and ensuring the utmost client satisfaction.

Ikem Isiekwena maintains a strong and collaborative relationship with the firm's most prominent clients, leveraging his expertise not only in his personal practice role but also in his capacity as a business leader. His dedication extends to leading and inspiring the firm's team of talented professionals, fostering a culture of excellence and innovation.

With over 19 years of experience, his practice areas span diverse industries such as FMCG, telecommunications, project finance, technology, and energy manifesting a deep understanding and unmatched skill set across sectors, including merger control, joint venture arrangements, regulatory investigations (concerning both horizontal and vertical pricing issues, as well as issues regarding the abuse of market power), and competition litigation.

His professional, business and community credentials are not limited to his role in SimmonsCooper but includes:
• Member of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA)
• Member NBA Section on Business Law.
• Non-executive Director & Chair of the Investments Committee at the Nigerian Sovereign Investment Authority (NSIA), shaping the nation's financial future through prudent investments.


Background
Ikem holds a Bachelor of Laws (Hons), University of Benin, Edo State, 2001 and was admitted into the Nigerian Bar in 2004. He joined the SimmonsCooper Partners in 2007 and was appointed as the Managing Partner in 2022. Ikem leads the Strategic Business Unit which encompasses Anti-trust/Competition, Mergers & Acquisition and Private Equity, and Energy practice areas. He holds an MBA from Robert Kennedy College, Switzerland, 2020, Postgraduate Diploma in Economics for Competition Law, Kings College London, 2012.


Experience & expertise

Selected Matters

Some of Ikem's key projects and contributions include:

• Lead, World Bank Consultant supporting the Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) on drafting and developing Nigeria’s competition law framework.

• Provided pioneer advisory, consultative and scholarly resources to support the development of various aspects of Consumer Protection and Competition/Anti-trust law in Nigeria.

• Advisory for an FMCG company on its US$14,000,000 acquisition of a non-alcoholic beverages brand.

• Successful minority acquisition for a financial/private equity investor in a telecommunications company, resulting in a 40% reduction in completion time.

• Advisory for the Debt Management Office of the Federal Government of Nigeria on its issuance of a non-interest financing N100,000,000,000 instrument (Sukuk) in the domestic debt market.
