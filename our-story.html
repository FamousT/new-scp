Our Story

Our Story

SimmonsCooper Partners (SCP) is a leading full-service law firm in Nigeria. We capture this in the SCP client service mantra: “Total Clients’ Solutions”.

SCP is the product of a cross-border fusion of the three diverse law practices of the founding partners streamed together to provide comprehensive and multi-jurisdictional legal services, of the highest possible legal standards.

The firm’s main offices are in Lagos and Abuja in Nigeria.

SCP provides a comprehensive and integrated range of transactional, litigation, advisory and support services. Some of our lawyers have multi-jurisdictional practice qualification spanning Nigeria and the United States. Our lawyers have delivered legal services in various capacities for over thirty years both within and outside Nigeria.

SCP is a member of the Globalaw Network – a group of over 100 independent law firms across 165 countries around the globe. As the only Nigerian member of the network, we enjoy a strong working relationship with all member law firms across the world, including the United Kingdom. With the benefit of these unique relationships, we are well suited to provide cross-border legal services to our clients. SCP is also a member of the Adam Global Network – the world’s largest multidisciplinary network of independent legal, accounting and consulting firms. As the only Nigerian legal firm member, we have leveraged on the network to provide value enhancing legal support to our multi-national clients across their various geographical locations. Our clientele ranges from individuals, through start-up and small businesses to multi-nationals and governments. Our clients are currently drawn from across Nigeria, West Africa, Southern Africa, the United States, Europe, South-East Asia and the Middle East.
